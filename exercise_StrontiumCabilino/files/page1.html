Tokyo (東京都)

  • Formerly known as Edo (江戸), it is Japan's capital. It is the country's most populous city standing at around 13 million people currently. Its urban area is the world's most populous as well.
  • It has an area of around 13,000 kilometers, and is in the east of the prefecture of Tokyo. This prefecture is in the region of Kanto (関東地方).
  • In 1868, it was declared the capital of the country, replacing the former imperial capital of Kyoto (京都).
  • The metropolis is divided into 23 special wards (特別区) in the east and 39 municipalities in the west.
  • Many landmarks lie in this city, especially the Tokyo Tower (東京タワー) completed in 1958. Some include the Tokyo Skytree (東京スカイツリー), Shibuya Crossing, and Ueno Park (上野公園).
