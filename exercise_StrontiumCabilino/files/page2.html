Yokohama (横浜市)

  • This city bears 2nd in population just behind Tokyo with around 4 million people currently. However, it is the most populous in its prefecture of Kanagawa (神奈川県)　on the region of Kanto (関東地方).
  • After the Edo period (江戸時代), it had became the first city in Japan to have a port after undergoing through isolation from foreign countries, commonly known as Sakoku (鎖国).
  • The most notable landmark is the Yokohama Landmark Tower (横浜ランドマークタワー), which is surprisingly seen in many Godzilla films.
