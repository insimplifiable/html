Osaka (大阪市)

  • This is the third most populous city, behind the major Kanto cities of Tokyo and Yokohama. And just like the aforementioned cities, it is the most populous city in its prefecture Osaka (大阪府) in the Kinki (近畿地方) or Kansai region (関西).
  • This city had become the first ever port city in Japan during the Koufun period (古墳時代), strengthening trade between neighboring countries and states.
  • One of the most notable tourist destinations in Osaka is Dotonbori (道頓堀), a popular area for entertainment. Another is the Osaka Castle (大阪城), which played an important role in Japanese history, specifically through its unification.
